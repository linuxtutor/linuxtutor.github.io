INTRODUCTION

I want to tell you a story—a different one. Not about Linus Torvalds and the creation of the Linux kernel or Richard Stallman's GNU Project. Those stories are well-documented in numerous Linux books. Instead, I want to share the story of how you reclaim control over your computer.

Linux is truly an extraordinary exception to the common saying, "you get what you pay for." Despite being freely available, Linux surpasses expectations in terms of performance, features, and reliability. Its cost-free nature does not diminish its capabilities in any way. Linux proves that exceptional quality and value can coexist without the need for a hefty price tag. Whether it's the impressive performance, extensive features, or unwavering reliability, Linux stands as a testament to the power of open-source software.

Starting a new skill can be overwhelming due to the vast amount of information available. It's frustrating when you encounter unexplained concepts and feel more confused than enlightened. However, "Linuxtutor" is here to help. No prior knowledge is assumed, and you'll be guided step by step with a logical approach. New concepts and jargon will be explained in plain language. Let's begin this journey together!
